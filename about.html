Crochet

I learned how to crochet about 10 years ago with the help of YouTube videos. I had previously learned to knit, but I always found it difficult to make anything other than scarves and nearly impossible to fix mistakes without starting over. Somebody told me crocheting was easier, so I started doing that and never got back to knitting. Crocheting is honestly one of the best things I've done. I like the feeling of having created something tangible, and the repetitive nature of crocheting is very calming.

Probably my favorite thing to crochet is hats. At this point I've probably made a hundred hats. Recently I made Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtle hats for my friend's twins and a Hello Kitty hat for my daughter. I like making amigurumi (crocheted small stuffed creatures), too. I'll post some pictures of my favorite projects.
