welcome

My name is Katie and I'm a data analyst, programmer, visualization enthusiast, crocheter and generalist. Data Chips is a venue to show charts that I've created and a place to work on my web development studies. I like to explore and make visuals on a variety of topics. My favorite areas of exploration include TV shows and general life observations. Please click the links below to see my portfolio.
